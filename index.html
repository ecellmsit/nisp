Our Concept

MSIT is a group of institution comprising of undergraduates from different domains. The ECell has spurred the initiative to transform lives and yield the support and guidance needed by the upcoming entrepreneurs. With the sole aim to let the concept of entrepreneurship burgeon and evolvement we are here to keep up its tradition of motivating students to come up with innovative ideas and groom them to the right note. For this we keep on organizing sessions by industry experts, entrepreneurs and internship drives to help everyone put their skills to practice.
